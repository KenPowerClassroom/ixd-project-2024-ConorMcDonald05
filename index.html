Introduction: Welcome to the introduction of my interaction design project focused on gamifying a learning app. our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users. gameification involves applying game design principles to non-game text such as education the objective is to increase user motivation engagement and enjoyment. this project involves various phases including a competitive analysis wire framing prototyping and usability testing the competitive analysis will help me identify strengths and weaknesses in the existing market and highlight areas we can improve. wire framing and prototyping allow us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app ensuring a smooth and enjoyable learning process. finally usability testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the apps usability and functionality the feedback will be used to refine and improve the app ensuring that the users needs are met overall our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable ultimately improving the users learning outcomes.
